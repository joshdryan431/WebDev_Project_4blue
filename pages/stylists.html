Meet Our Stylists

Get to know our talented team of professionals.

Our talented team is dedicated to making you look and feel your best. Each stylist brings their own specialty — from precision cutting to advanced colour work and restorative treatments.

Joanne

Specialty: Precision cuts, modern styles, and bridal hair.

Joanne has 10+ years of experience creating flattering cuts for every face shape. She loves working with textured hair and is known for her detailed finishing.

Dustin

Specialty: Restorative treatments, keratin smoothing, and scalp care.

Dustin focuses on hair health—offering tailored treatment plans to repair and strengthen damaged hair. He'll recommend the best in-salon and at-home care.

Sarah

Specialty: Colour, balayage, and creative tones.

Sarah is our colour specialist, blending light and depth to create natural-looking colour and lived-in highlights. She loves bold colour transformations too.
