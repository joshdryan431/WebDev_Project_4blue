Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers to our most commonly asked questions — quick, clear and friendly.

1. How far in advance should I book an appointment?
We recommend booking **1–2 weeks in advance** to secure your preferred stylist and time. We do accept last-minute bookings when possible—call or use our online booking system to check availability.
2. What should I do before a colour appointment?
Arrive with **clean, dry hair** that isn’t freshly washed the same day. Avoid heavy oils or styling products as they can affect colour processing. If you’re unsure, book a short consultation beforehand.
3. Do you offer consultations for major changes (colour or cut)?
Yes — we offer **complimentary consultations** for major transformations, extensions, and corrective colour. This helps us align on expectations and recommend the best approach for your hair goals.
4. Which brands and products do you use?
We use trusted, high-performance lines that prioritise hair health and sustainability, including **Kevin Murphy**, **K18**, and other premium professional ranges — chosen to deliver lasting, healthy results.
5. Do you have treatments for damaged or fragile hair?
Absolutely. We provide tailored repair treatments such as **K18**, keratin smoothing, and deep-repair masks to restore strength, shine, and manageability. Ask your stylist for a recommendation during your appointment.
6. What is your cancellation policy?
We kindly request **at least 24 hours’ notice** for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the time slot to another client. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.
